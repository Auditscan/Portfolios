Process

Process

Our systematic, tailored process. Scroll to advance through each phase.

01 ⚑ Phase I

Request & Vision

Like all great adventures do, we must first set the scene.

You can do this smoothly and efficiently via the project request. The information you provide really helps us understand whether we're the right team for your creative vision. If we believe our skillset is certain to do the project justice, we'll arrange a video call to meet you and your team. We strive to get back to every project request within 48 hours.

02 ⚑ Phase II

Meeting & Scope

Next, we introduce our heroic protagonists.

The main aim of this initial video call is to establish trust and understanding on both sides. We'll discuss precise goals, timeframes & requirements, clarify any questions and plot out a clear and actionable plan going forwards. Our favourite part of these calls is meeting founders passionate about their brand, with grand ideas for scaling it.

03 ⚑ Phase III

Proposal & Timeline

Once a deal is struck, the quest begins.

Once we have a clear understanding of your project scope, we'll create an individual proposal for you. We love working closely with internal design teams, or fleshing out compelling branding & storytelling ideas ourselves. We charge on a per-project basis, rather than by the hour. This keeps everything transparent and straightforward, as you have a comprehensive overview of the costs and timeline.

04 ⚑ Phase IV

Design & Construction

Our teams embrace the final challenge.

If both parties are content with the proposal, we line up the playlist and get to work. We often begin with rough wireframes and UX flows before moving onto the more rigorous code-crunching, copywriting and design polish. You'll always be kept up to date — clear, responsive communication is at the heart of everything we do.

05 ⚑ Phase V

Launch & Handover

Every great adventure must come to an end.

Before launching your store, we put it through a series of stress-tests and optimisation checks to prove its speed, quality & conversion potency. We host on fast, reliable infrastructure and hand over clean documentation, so your team can run with it confidently. We want launch day to be a celebration, not a panic — and we stick around afterwards to make sure it stays that way.
